Facts:

1. Their simple design has made them the perfect symbol for many organizations that plan on saving the ocean.

2. The shell has been in many species families for generations, and Turtles are one of the most well known. This simple defence tactic allows them to survive longer than normal prey.

3. One of the most famous turtles was a small one named Hope. Her heart was exposed and forced to go through many struggles to keep herself alive with her owner. She has become a symbol for hope for all sea life once she came into the public eye.
