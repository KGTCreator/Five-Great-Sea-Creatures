Koi

Intro: Koi are simple creatures. They take what they are given, they accept what they don't expect, and are some of the most harmless creatures known to Earth.

Facts:

1. These fish are most likely seen in ponds at hotels and stores, two examples being Congress Hall and the Fulton Steamboat Inn.

2. If someone is born the same day as a koi, and lives to 80 years old, the koi will be in that koi's life for almost one third of that person's life.

3. Koi are a type of mordern carp that has some variations native to Japan.
